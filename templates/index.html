Help Section

Unauthenticated users have only access tu Surah 3. Click on Cancel when the login pop-up appears.

Enter the starting and ending indices to extract Quranic text. You can leave any index empty.

  • Initial empty indexes will be automatically filled with the first value.
  • End empty indexes will be automatically filled with the last value of the corresponding sequence.
  • Block indicates a letterblock, i.e., a group of connected letters. For instance, the word ٱلرَّحۡمَٰنِ contains three letterblocks: (1) ٱ (2) لرَّ and (3) حۡمَٰنِ.
  • When verse markers are activated, you can use the up and down arrows at the right of the textarea to navigate to the previous or next surah.

Switches:

  • Graphemes shows all diacritics. Archigraphemes shows only the consonantal skeleton, without any vocalic or consonantal diacritic.
  • Words show spaces between logical words. Blocks shows spaces between words and also between letterblocks, recreating the way script is presented in manuscripts.
  • Arabic shows the text in the Arabic writing system. Latin shows all the graphemes in a pure transliteration (see the mapping below).
  • Verse markers show the sura and verse numbers, whereas block of text omits them.
    • When Arabic is selected, suras are indicated at the beginning in this format: "﴿ صوره ١ ﴾". Verses are marked with the expected verse mark.
    • When Latin is selected, after each verse both sura and verse are indicated with the format "1:8".

Arabic Latin Mapping:
